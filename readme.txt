Receive payments directly to your store through the Vodacom Mozambique M-Pesa API. 

== Description ==
M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce plugin allows you to receive payments directly to your store through the Vodacom Mozambique M-Pesa API.
Through M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce, shoppers at your store can purchase products or services at your store using a phone number associated with the M-Pesa account.

 
== Installation ==
 
1. Login to your WordPress Dashboard
2. Click on 'Plugins > Add New' from the left menu.
3. In the search box type M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce.
4. Click on Install Now on M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce to install the plugin on your site and activate the plugin.
5. Click on 'WooCommerce > Settings' from the left menu and click the 'Checkout' tab.
6. Click on the M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce from the available Checkout 
7. Configure your M-Pesa Payment Gateway for WooCommerce settings using credentials available on https://developer.mpesa.vm.co.mz/
