Receive payments directly to your store through the Vodacom Mozambique M-Pesa API. 

== Description ==
THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway plugin allows you to receive payments directly to your store through the Vodacom Mozambique M-Pesa API.
Through THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway, shoppers at your store can purchase products or services at your store using a phone number associated with the M-Pesa account.

 
== Installation ==
###Automatic Installation
1. Login to your WordPress Dashboard
2. Click on 'Plugins > Add New' from the left menu.
3. In the search box type THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway.
4. Click on Install Now on THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway to install the plugin on your site and activate the plugin.
5. Click on 'WooCommerce > Settings' from the left menu and click the 'Checkout' tab.
6. Click on the THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway from the available Checkout 
7. Configure your THC M-Pesa Payment Gateway settings using credentials available on https://developer.mpesa.vm.co.mz/
